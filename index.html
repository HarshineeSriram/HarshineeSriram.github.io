Who am I?

Hi! I am Harshinee Sriram. I am an incoming MSc Computer Science at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver where I will be specializing in Computational Intelligence!

Currently, I am an Outreachy intern at the Wikimedia Foundation. Previously, I have been a Visiting Research Scholar at the University of Auckland, New Zealand, a Remote Research Intern at the Guidance, Control, and Decision Systems lab at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc Bengaluru), and an Artificial Intelligence Intern at Tech Mahindra Headquarters in Bengaluru (India) as well as at the National University of Singapore (Singapore).
